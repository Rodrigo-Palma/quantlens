# Quant & B3 Glossary

## RSI (Relative Strength Index)
A momentum oscillator bounded between 0 and 100 that measures the speed and
magnitude of recent price changes. Readings above 70 are commonly described as
overbought and readings below 30 as oversold. RSI is computed with Wilder's
smoothing over a look-back period (14 sessions by default).

## Momentum
The total return of an asset over a trailing window (20 sessions by default).
Positive momentum indicates an uptrend; negative momentum a downtrend. Momentum
is descriptive of past behavior and is not a forecast.

## Annualized Volatility
The standard deviation of daily returns scaled to a yearly figure (multiplied by
the square root of 252 trading days). Higher volatility means larger price swings
and, generally, higher risk.

## B3 (Brasil, Bolsa, Balcão)
The Brazilian stock exchange, based in São Paulo. Tickers use a numeric suffix
(e.g. PETR4, VALE3) and the Yahoo Finance convention appends ".SA"
(e.g. PETR4.SA).

## Covered Call
An options income strategy where an investor who owns 100 shares sells a call
option against them, collecting a premium and capping upside above the strike.

## Cash-Secured Put
Selling a put option while reserving the full cash needed to buy the shares if
assigned. It generates premium income and can be a way to enter a position at a
discount, with risk comparable to owning the stock outright.
